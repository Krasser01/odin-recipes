Butter Brioche




Butterbrioche. The word alone leaves me in the 7th. Heavens float. The fluffy-fine briochelaib with Tangzhong simply convinces on all levels. Tangzhong is a kind of dough and simply brings even more liquid and elasticity to the dough. Perfect for brioche and for me just an indispensable part.

Preperation

    Dough (Tangzhong)

    • 100g whole milk
    • whole milk
        • universal or W700(T550)

    Main dough

    • 35 g sugar
    • 370 g wheat flour
        • universal or W700(T550)
    • 7 g dry
    • 100 g whole milk, cold
    • 2 eggs, cold
    • 10 g salt
    • 60 g butter, soft

    To spread

    • 1 egg
    • 1 tbsp whole milk
    • Butter
        • for the finished bread

Butter Brioche: Step by Step

  1. First mix milk with flour in a small pan and then heat it at medium to high temperature, stirring constantly, for 1-2 minutes until you first have a creamy type of porridge or roux in front of you - our dough, the so-called Tangzhong.

    This now comes on a plate and has to cool down completely. By the way, this works best in the freezer!

  2. Then mix flour, sugar and dry water in the bowl of your food processor and clamp into the food processor.

    Turn on the machine at low speed and pour in the cold milk.

  3. Then beat one egg after another.

    Now add the cooled Tangzhong.

    The speed is increased by one level.
